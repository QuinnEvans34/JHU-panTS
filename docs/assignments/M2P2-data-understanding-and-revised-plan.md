# M2P2: Data Understanding & Revised Plan (Presentation)

> Due Sunday by 11:59pm · Points: 30 · Submit via GitHub · Available until Jul 19

## Overview

Present the dataset to the class as if briefing a business stakeholder before a modeling decision. Show that you understand the data deeply enough to make confident, defensible choices about what to build next. Peers will challenge the findings and the revised plan.

## Instructions

Presenter — deliver a 12-minute presentation covering:

1. What the data looks like: source, shape, quality issues found and resolved.
2. The 3 most important EDA findings and what they mean for the ML approach.
3. Any changes to the original problem framing or model plan based on what was found.
4. The finalized Core Requirements and 5-week schedule.

Be ready for 10 minutes of Q&A (expect questions on data-quality decisions, feature choices, and whether the revised plan is realistic).

Audience — same structured engagement as Week 1. Pay particular attention to:
- Whether the presenter's EDA findings actually justify their modeling decisions.
- Whether the revised requirements and schedule are specific and achievable.

## Deliverables

- Presenter: `data-understanding-report.md` and `eda-notebook.ipynb` via GitHub.
- Audience: `audience-notes-week2.md`.
